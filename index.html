Clinical Guidance & Protocols

Review measurement instructions and descriptors before performing clinical assessment.

  • Mandatory Completion: Required for all ongoing wound interventions, reviewed at least every 7 days.
  • Dimensions: Measure in cm/mm using a single-use disposable tape measure. Do not reuse multi-patient tape measures.
    • Length: Head to toe
    • Width: Right to left
    • Depth: Deepest point perpendicular to surface
  • Tracking & Undermining: Use clock-face coordinate system (12 o'clock aligns with head/top of wound, 3 o'clock to patient's right side).
  • Cavity Wounds: Gently and loosely insert dressing; do not pack tightly to avoid forming plugs that block exudate drainage.
  • Two-Week Rule: If wound has not progressed within two weeks, refer to relevant wound specialists.
